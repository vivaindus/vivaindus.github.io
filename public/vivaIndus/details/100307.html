About iSlim

iSlim (often presented as **iSlim Flat Tummies**) is a botanical weight-management bar formulated with a synergistic blend of phyto-extracts, protein, fibre and nutrients intended to help support healthy weight goals. Its composition is designed to help fight cravings, boost metabolism, support fat burning, and assist in flattening the tummy when used alongside balanced diet and lifestyle habits.
